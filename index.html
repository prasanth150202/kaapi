The Problem

The Real Cost of Bad Office Beverages

Most companies overpay for inconsistent, unhygienic, vendor-dependent coffee. Here's what it's actually costing you.

Hidden vendor markups

Caterers charge ₹25–₹50 per cup — whether your employees drink it or not. Wastage and idle billing add up to lakhs annually.

Taste varies every day

Manual preparation depends on who made it today. Employees compare each cup — and complain when it's off.

Hygiene blind spots

Chai wallahs and contract staff bring uncontrolled hygiene risks — a growing concern for hospitals, labs, and ISO-certified facilities.

No consumption data

You have no visibility into cups served, cost per cup, or peak hours. Every month is a guess on the vendor invoice.

FAQ

Common Questions

How is KaapiVend different from Nescafé or generic vending machines?

Generic machines use factory pre-mix powder from unknown sources. KaapiVend uses Arabica beans from our own Thriveni Brooklyn Estate — the same beans served in our cafés rated 4.4★ across Tamil Nadu. The difference is unmistakable in every cup.

What beverages can one machine dispense?

KS X - 5 and KS X - 10 offer filter coffee (strong/light/black), brewed tea (strong/light/black), hot milk, and hot water. KS B - 5 adds cappuccino, latte, and espresso via a touch screen. Specialty mixes like ginger tea, elaichi tea, sukku kaapi, and badam mix are also available.

Who handles maintenance and refills?

We do. A dedicated relationship manager handles all refills, servicing, and breakdown support under our AMC programme. You receive a monthly consumption report and have a single point of contact for everything.

What is the approximate cost per cup?

Typically ₹6–₹10 per cup depending on volume and model — compared to ₹25–₹50 with vendor-made beverages. Our free demo or quote gives you an exact figure tailored to your specific setup and headcount.

Can we trial the machine before committing?

Yes — qualifying companies this month get a free 7-day trial machine. Fill the form and our team will assess your eligibility during the first call. No deposit or commitment required for the trial.

Is there a minimum contract period?

Flexible terms are available based on your company size and requirements. Our specialist will walk you through options during the consultation — no lock-in pressure on the first call.

Which cities do you currently serve?

We currently serve Salem, Coimbatore, Hosur, and Dharmapuri. We are expanding rapidly across Tamil Nadu. Contact us to check availability in your city — we may be closer than you think.
